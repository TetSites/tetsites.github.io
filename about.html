About Tetsites

Tetsites is a free subdomain website hosting service designed to help people get their own personal website path under the tetsites.github.io domain.

Users can contribute by submitting pull requests to create their own folders, hosting their custom websites easily without needing to buy a domain or manage hosting.

Our goal is to provide a simple, community-driven platform where everyone can share their projects, portfolios, or personal pages in a hassle-free way.

For more details on contributing, please visit the Contributing page.
